Welcome to Coding for Beginners!

This website aims to introduce beginners to the world of coding. Whether you're just starting out or looking for resources to further your skills, you're in the right place.

Why Learn Coding?

Coding is not just about creating software or building websites. It's about problem solving, logical thinking, and turning ideas into reality. In the modern world, coding is a valuable skill that can open up numerous opportunities in various sectors.

Getting Started

If you're brand new to coding, we recommend starting with HTML and CSS. These are the foundational languages for web development and are relatively easy to learn.
